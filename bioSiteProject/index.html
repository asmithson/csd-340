Welcome to Rose T's Story

From handling hazard calls to highlighting hair — this is the story of transformation. Working in the office of a pest control company, Rose T spent their days managing problems from a distance. The calls were urgent, the work was steady, but the results? Distant and unfulfilling. Watching a colleague celebrate ten years in that same role made one thing clear — that wasn’t the future Rose T wanted.
So they left. And in doing so, chose something brave: creativity.
Enrolling in beauty school wasn’t just about changing careers — it was about stepping into a space where color, expression, and transformation are tangible. Now, Rose T brings that spark to a small, independent salon where learning and connection go hand in hand.
This site highlights their journey: the courage to begin again, the color they bring to their work, and the hobbies and dreams that make them who they are.

Transformation Journey

Behind the desk at a pest control office — solving problems, but never seeing the results.

Working behind the scenes in a pest control office meant solving problems — but never witnessing solutions. “A guy sets the traps and you hope they work,” Rose T shared, summing up the disconnect that defined that job. The lack of vibrancy in those days — and in this photo — says it all.

Nervous but excited — Rose T stepping into beauty school, ready for change.

As beauty school began, color and confidence returned to Rose T’s life. With every foil placed and formula mixed, the transformation wasn’t just happening on clients — it was happening in Rose T, too. School became more than education — it became empowerment.

Cat therapy: a well-deserved break after a day of learning.
Graduation day — Rose T steps confidently into the beauty industry with fresh skills and new beginnings.

Rose T stepped into the beauty industry with a fresh license, nerves, and newfound confidence. “Even though it’s hard, and it is hard, I know I’m just beginning,” they said. The sparkle others now see isn’t just on the outside — it’s the glow of someone who finally chose themselves.
